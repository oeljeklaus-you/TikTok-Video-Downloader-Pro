TikTok Video Downloader Pro

Last Updated: May 22, 2026

🔒 Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to TikTok Video Downloader Pro ("we," "our," or "the Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

By installing and using this Extension, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't do:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No Browsing History: We do not track or record your browsing history.
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics tools to track your usage.
  • No Cookies: We do not set or use cookies.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not share data with third parties because we don't collect any data.

3. Data Storage

The Extension stores the following information locally on your device only:

3.1 Download Queue

  • Video IDs and URLs for videos you choose to download
  • Download progress and status
  • Video metadata (creator name, caption, view counts, etc.)

3.2 User Settings

  • Language preference
  • Download concurrency settings
  • Filename template preferences
  • Folder organization settings

3.3 Download History

  • List of previously downloaded videos (to prevent duplicates)
  • Download timestamps

📍 Storage Location: All data is stored using Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local) and remains on your device. This data is never transmitted to any external server.

4. Permissions Explained

The Extension requires the following permissions to function:

4.1 Storage Permission

Why we need it: To save your download queue, settings, and history locally on your device.

What we do with it: Store data in Chrome's local storage. No data leaves your device.

4.2 Downloads Permission

Why we need it: To download TikTok videos to your computer.

What we do with it: Initiate and manage video downloads through Chrome's download API.

4.3 Active Tab Permission

Why we need it: To scan TikTok pages for videos when you click the extension icon.

What we do with it: Read video information from the current TikTok.com tab only when you explicitly request it.

4.4 Host Permissions (tiktok.com)

Why we need it: To access TikTok.com pages and extract video information.

What we do with it: Read video metadata from TikTok pages. We only access TikTok.com domains, not other websites.

5. How We Use Information

The information stored locally on your device is used solely for:

  • Managing your download queue
  • Preventing duplicate downloads
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Providing download history

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Period.

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect any information, there is nothing to share.

7. Data Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • All data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage API
  • No data transmission to external servers
  • No network requests except to TikTok.com for downloading videos
  • No authentication or login required

8. Data Retention and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

8.1 Automatic Deletion

When you uninstall the Extension, all locally stored data is automatically removed by Chrome.

8.2 Manual Deletion

You can manually delete data at any time:

  • Clear Queue: Use the "Cancel All" button in the Queue tab
  • Clear History: Use the "Clear History" button in the Settings tab
  • Reset Settings: Uninstall and reinstall the Extension
